**ARC RUNNER **


An endless space runner game built on Arc, where every run is on-chain and every score is permanent.

About the Game

ARC RUNNER is a simple but addictive endless runner set in space. Connect your wallet, pay a small fee to play, and dodge as many asteroids as you can. How far can you go before you crash?

There are no fake high scores here — every run and every score is written on-chain, forever. What you see is what actually happened.

**How to Play**


Connect your wallet — supports MetaMask and Rabby
Pay to play — each run costs 0.01 USDC in gas
Dodge asteroids — steer your ship and avoid obstacles as long as possible
Collect coins — grab Arc-logo coins along the way for extra points
Survive — the longer you last, the higher your score
Your final score is recorded on-chain, permanently


**Why On-Chain Scores?**

Traditional leaderboards can be reset, edited, or faked. In ARC RUNNER, every score lives on the blockchain — transparent, verifiable, and impossible to fake. Your run is your run, forever.

**Requirements**


MetaMask or Rabby wallet (browser extension or mobile)
A small amount of USDC to cover the play fee
